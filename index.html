About us

Welcome! This path finder was created with York University in residence students in mind. With the current restrictions in place due to Covid-19, we thought it would be nice to have a go-to list of essential services that students might need. Our map is based on the UN SDG, Good Health and wellbeing. We have therefore included healthcare centers and locations to get food.

New international students and those from other provinces may not have had the opportunity to explore their surroundings due to a lack of volunteers on campus, Covid Curfews, and a ton of school work .

This pathfinder can be seen as a one-stop shop to locate some essential services. The map provides the shortest path to various Tim Hortons locations on and off campus, it provides a list of the closest grocery stores, hospitals/ medical centres, dental offices, and Covid-19 Assessment Centres.

Some of the Test centres are located at Shoppers Drug Mart so that is an additional advantage for all. All starting points are centered around York University. A student can choose their residence from the list and then select their destination.

The knowledge required to create this webpage stems from the first few weeks of our ESSE 2615 class (Introduction to Geomatics Engineering). As we explore the various areas of geomatics engineering, our first project was mainly focused on building/ designing a web application.

Building on the knowledge gained, we were able to design this webpage from a template, edit and compile our CSS files and include the google map, which requires a Google API key.
